Why Chant?

Chanting the Hare Krishna Mahamantra is the most effective way to purify the heart and mind, leading to spiritual enlightenment and liberation from the cycle of birth and death.

Chanting this mantra will help you to get rid of all your sins and will help you to attain the supreme goal of life i.e. going back to godhead

Things you can do to reach to Shri Golok Dham are:

  • Chant 16 rounds daily
  • Perform Arti
  • Read Scriptures by Srila Prabhupada

Here If you want to know How to Chant

  1. Sit in front of Tulasi Maharani
  2. Take a Japa Mala in your hand
  3. Start chanting Hare Krishna Mahamantra
  4. Chant 108 times on one bead
  5. After completing 108 times, move to next bead by reciting Panchtatva mantra
